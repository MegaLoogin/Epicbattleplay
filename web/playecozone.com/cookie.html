1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember your preferences, improve your experience, and provide insights into user behavior.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website to help us improve its functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable enhanced functionality, such as remembering your preferences and login details.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Provide essential website functionality.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of the website and your user experience. Instructions for managing cookies can be found in your browser's help section or settings menu.
